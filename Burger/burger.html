Burger

Burgers are the only food that Americans understand

Ingredients

  • 0.5 lbs of Ground Beef
  • Burger Bun
  • 1 slice of American Cheese
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat your skillet by setting it to medium
  2. Slice your onion, tomato, and lettuce
  3. Form a patty using the ground beef
  4. Place the patty onto the skillet
  5. Flip the patty once your desired doneness is reached
  6. Once both sides of the patty are cooked to your deisred doneness, place the patty onto a bottom burger bun
  7. Add a slice of american cheese ontop of the burger patty
  8. Add a slice on onion ontop of the american cheese
  9. Add a slice of lettuce ontop of the onion
  10. Add a slice of tomato ontop of the lettuce
  11. Place the top burger bun ontop of the lettuce
