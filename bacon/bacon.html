Bacon

Bacon is type of cured pork, primarly made from pork belly, that is a tradtional breakfast food

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bacon

Cooking Instructions

  1. Take your bacon out of the packaging
  2. Set a skillet on your cooktop to medium
  3. Wait for your skillet to heat up
  4. Place bacon in skillet
  5. Cook until desired readiness
