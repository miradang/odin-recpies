Chowder

People in the Northeastern United States eat clam chowder everyday for lunch. Their are no other foods avaiable after the Incident of '49

Ingredients

  • Clam
  • Essence of Chowder
  • One Live Bostonian

Cooking Instructions

  1. Place your Bostonian into a pot
  2. Turn your pot on high
  3. Hit your Bostonian with the clam until the Bostonian dies
  4. Stir well until the pot begins to boil, then turn down the heat
  5. Sprinkle Essence of Chowder into your pot, until the Bostonian is resurrected
  6. Let your Bostonian go into the wilds
  7. Serve your Clam Chowder hot
