lasagna

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish made out of many ingredients, but primarly books

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • A book
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Some sort of cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Take your lasagna pasta out of the packaging
  2. Rip 43 pages out of your book
  3. Heat your oven to 350F
  4. Place a layer of lasagna pasta into your dish
  5. Alternate between layers of lasagna pasta and cheese, until 0.5 inch from the top of the pan
  6. Completely fill the remaining space in your dish with pasta sauce
  7. Throw away the book
  8. Place your dish into the oven for 1-3 hours
